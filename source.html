Blackjack

Info

In this simulator the rules are of standard blackjack, where you play solo against the dealer with a 52 card deck that excludes jokers. This simulation is made with one deck of cards that is reshuffled between every round of blackjack and with every card played it is removed from the virtual deck meaning it cannot be played during that round. The aim of the game is to be closest to 21 without going over it, as the player, you will start with 2 cards and decide to hit(take another card at the risk of going over 21) or stand(pass the turn onto the dealer and hope that he will not get closer to 21 than you). If you do get over 21, as the player you immediatly lose. Each card has a value, each card from 2-10 included holds the same value as written and the card, the jack, queen and king are worth ten and an ace is either 1 or 11 points.
